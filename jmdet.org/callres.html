Call For Research Papers


Submission open for January - June 2014

The JMDET receives a very large number of quality papers for publication . JMDET focuses on cutting edge multi disciplinary research and publishes novel research papers, articles, case studies, review papers, research project synopsis, recently completed Ph.D./M.Tech. thesis reports and reviews of books in the fields of engineering, science and technology. The journal serves as a window on latest trends and developments in various disciplines of engineering and technology for students and researchers.

We would like to invite you to contribute a Research Paper for publication in JMDET( Vol 8 Issue 1). Papers published in JMDET will receive very high publicity and acquire very high reputation.
